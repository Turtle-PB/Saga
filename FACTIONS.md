## Confirmed factions and power blocs

### Appleomia

A walled digital kingdom associated with Gatekeeper defenses and the Architect of Cupertino.  
Evidence: `README.md:26`, `index.html:122-137`

### The Open Core

An opposing force to Appleomia that frames the first breach as liberation from a closed system.  
Evidence: `index.html:131`

### SLCK-9 / the rogue Slack protocol

Presented as a once-allied communication system that seeks total control over channels and frequency.  
Evidence: `index.html:147-150`

### Echo civilization remnants

Represented through Daemon-7 and echo entities: dead processes or civilizations persisting as residual data.  
Evidence: `index.html:164-167`

### RISC-V Coalition

The open-hardware side of Arc IV's architecture war.  
Evidence: `index.html:182`

### ARM Dominion

The opposing military architecture power in Arc IV.  
Evidence: `README.md:29`, `index.html:179-185`

### The Architect's Ledger

Not clearly a faction; currently treated as a singular reality-governing entity or artifact around which factions have fought.  
Evidence: `README.md:30`, `index.html:198-202`
