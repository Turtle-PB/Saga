# GLOSSARY

See also: [LORE.md](LORE.md), [STORY_ARCS.md](STORY_ARCS.md)

## Terms

### Arc

A major chapter of the multiverse conflict, each with its own realm, enemies, boss, and victory text.  
Evidence: `README.md:22-31`, `index.html:119-206`

### The Breach

The active rupture that opens traversal into the current game conflict.  
Evidence: `README.md:60-82`, `index.html:75-77`

### WSL Gateway

The interdimensional bridge that links otherwise incompatible digital civilizations.  
Evidence: `README.md:18-20`, `index.html:76`

### Data Fragments

The current build's score pickups, which also restore a small amount of health.  
Evidence: `index.html:502-535`, `index.html:641-646`

### Echo Lattice

The dead-data realm associated with Daemon-7 and Arc III.  
Evidence: `index.html:165-168`

### Open Core

An anti-Appleomia force associated with breach and liberation rhetoric.  
Evidence: `index.html:131`

### Architect's Ledger

The final living codex whose control appears to affect causality itself.  
Evidence: `README.md:30`, `index.html:198-202`
