## Current questline structure

The implemented build presents one linear macro-quest: survive each arc, defeat its boss, and advance to the next convergence point.

## Arc objectives currently confirmed

1. **Appleomia** — Destroy Gatekeeper defenses, collect fragments, survive the walled garden.  
   Evidence: `index.html:131-134`
2. **Slack Rogue** — Fight through notification storms and collapse SLCK-9's fragments.  
   Evidence: `index.html:148-151`
3. **Protocol of Echoes** — Escape dead-data recursion by defeating echo threats and Daemon-7.  
   Evidence: `index.html:165-168`
4. **ARM Wars** — End the architecture war by destroying the Dominion core authority.  
   Evidence: `index.html:182-185`
5. **Architect's Ledger** — Defeat the final guardians and contest the laws of reality.  
   Evidence: `index.html:198-202`

## Not currently implemented

- Branching quests
- Side quests
- Reputation-gated quests
- Persisted quest logs
